Estimates of Polarization Angles and Cross-Polar Efficiency

In the pager below, I show this year's polarization angles (in terms of $\phi$, not χ) and cross-polar efficiency per channel and per-observation. I include the data I took in 2018 for comparison.

The data between 2018 and 2022 are very consistent -- the major differences (~0.1deg shift, higher variance) can be well explained by the 2018 data having a bad tiltmeter calibration . Other than that, we have a confirmation that the polarization properties over the years have remained relatively unchanged (even after swapping out a window).
