Comparison between 2018 and 2022 RPS-derived Polarization Properties

2022 Jun 01 — J. Cornelison

The following pager shows histograms of the per-detector RPS-derived polarization angles and cross-polarization efficiency for the 2018 and 2022 measurement seasons. The pol angle and xpol for a given detector were averaged over all available measurements from that year weighted by the fit uncertainty returned by matmin.m. Only detectors available in both years were used in the histograms in either year. I also show the difference between the parameter estimates between the two years.
